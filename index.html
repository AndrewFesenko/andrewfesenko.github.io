About The App

We are your go-to marketplace for buying and selling used goods exclusively for students.
Founded with the mission to make college life more affordable and sustainable,
DormDeals connects students across campuses, allowing them to sell everything from
textbooks and electronics to furniture and dorm essentials.

Security

Your safety is our priority. DormDeals is exclusively for your campus community, ensuring that every user. Enjoy peace of mind knowing that you're trading with trusted peers.

Convenience

Say goodbye to complicated communication. With our in-app messaging feature, you can easily chat with buyers and sellers directly within the app, making transactions smoother and faster.

Affordability

Get the best deals on everything you need for college life. DormDeals allows students to buy and sell at prices that fit a student budget, making essential items more accessible and affordable for everyone.
