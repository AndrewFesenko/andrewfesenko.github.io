ABOUT

Who We Are

Welcome to DormDeals! We are your go-to marketplace for buying and selling used goods, exclusively for students, by students. Founded with the mission to make college life more affordable and sustainable, DormDeals connects students across campuses, allowing them to sell everything from textbooks and electronics to furniture and dorm essentials.

Our first beta launch will occur close to home, exclusively for students from the University of Pennsylvania

FEATURES

Why You'll Love DormDeals

Discover the key features that make DormDeals the ultimate marketplace for college students. From ensuring secure transactions to promoting sustainability, DormDeals is designed with your needs in mind.

Security

Your safety is our priority. DormDeals is exclusively for your campus community, ensuring that every user. Enjoy peace of mind knowing that you're trading with trusted peers.

Convenience

Say goodbye to complicated communication. With our in-app messaging feature, you can easily chat with buyers and sellers directly within the app, making transactions smoother and faster.

Sustainability

Help reduce waste and promote sustainability by reusing items. DormDeals encourages the reuse of textbooks, furniture, and other goods, keeping them out of landfills and giving them a second life.

Affordability

Get the best deals on everything you need for college life. DormDeals allows students to buy and sell at prices that fit a student budget, making essential items more accessible and affordable for everyone.
